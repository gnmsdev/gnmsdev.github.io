Privacy Policy

Definition

This privacy policy is designed to provide clarity about personal information and data collected from users.

Collected Information

GNMŞ uses the AdMob ad network (a third-party service) to monetize their apps. AdMob can collect information in accordance with their own Privacy Policy.

Firebase and Google Analytics can collect information in accordance with their own Privacy Policy.

Other

Other than that, GNMŞ currently does not collect any type of information or data.
